Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

This recipe is one that has been passed down in my family for 4 generations. It was made by my great-grandmother on my mother's mother's side. It utilizes 2 cheeses to create some of the great gooyness ever made!

Ingredents

  • 24 oz. of macaroni noodles
  • 33.1 oz. of cream of mushroom soup
  • 33.1 oz. of whole milk
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 16 oz. of cheddar cheese
  • 16 oz. of mozzarella cheese
  • 2 generous seasonings of salt
  • 2 generous seasonings of pepper

Steps

  1. first layer of macaroni
  2. first layer of cheese mix
  3. season wih salt and pepper
  4. second layer of macaroni
  5. second layer of cheese mix
  6. season with salt and pepper
  7. season with paprika
  8. 2 tbsp of chopped butter
  9. preheat oven to 350 degrees
  10. cook for 45 minutes
